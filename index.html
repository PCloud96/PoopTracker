About

Poop Tracker is designed to help you understand your digestive health. Whether you're managing IBS, preparing for a doctor's visit, or simply curious about your patterns, Poop Tracker makes it easy to log, track, and analyze your bowel movements.

All data stays on your device. Your health information is never uploaded to any server.
