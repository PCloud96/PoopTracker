Privacy Policy

Poop Tracker: Bowel Health Log

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Overview

Poop Tracker ("the App") is developed by Cloud Motion Lab. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Data Collection

Poop Tracker is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your bowel movement logs, health data, and personal information are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

What We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Health or medical data
  • Location data
  • Browsing history
  • Contacts or photos

On-Device Data

All bowel movement entries, symptoms, and health logs you create are stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export a PDF report and share it yourself.

Apple HealthKit

If you grant permission, the App may read from and write to Apple HealthKit. HealthKit data is governed by Apple's privacy policies and is never accessed by Cloud Motion Lab. HealthKit data is not used for advertising or shared with third parties.

Face ID / Touch ID

The App offers an optional biometric lock feature using Face ID or Touch ID. Biometric data is processed entirely by Apple's LocalAuthentication framework on your device. We never receive, store, or have access to your biometric information.

Advertising

The free version of the App may display ads provided by Unity Ads. Unity Ads may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policy. Pro subscribers do not see any ads. You can review Unity's privacy policy at unity.com/legal/privacy-policy.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store payment information. Transaction records are managed by Apple and accessible through your Apple ID account.

Analytics

We may use Apple's built-in App Analytics to understand general usage patterns (such as app opens and screen views). This data is anonymized and aggregated by Apple. No personally identifiable information is included.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the App, please contact us so we can address the concern.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
